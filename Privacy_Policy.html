📋 Privacy Policy

AnfieldVoice Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Red Cape Technologies (Pty) Ltd (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the AnfieldVoice estate management platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and delete your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary for estate management:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and phone number
  • Apartment data — your unit number, building, and move-in/out dates
  • Role assignments — your role(s) within the system (resident, property administrator, etc.)
  • Activity logs — administrative actions you perform are recorded in our immutable audit trail

We do not collect location data, device identifiers, photos, or contacts from your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and grant appropriate access to estate features
  • Enable resident communications (gate calls, visitor management)
  • Maintain an immutable audit trail for estate security and compliance
  • Facilitate tenant management by property administrators

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only within your estate:

  • Body corporate administrators can view estate-wide resident data
  • Property administrators can view residents of apartments they manage
  • Security personnel can view resident names and unit numbers for gate access

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you maintain an active account. Audit trail entries are retained indefinitely for estate compliance and security purposes, even after account deletion.

5. Your Rights (POPIA)

Under South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate information
  • Deletion — delete your account and anonymize your personal data
  • Objection — object to the processing of your data

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. When you delete your account:

  • Your name, email, and phone number are permanently anonymized
  • Your password is invalidated — you can no longer log in
  • You are removed from all apartment resident rosters
  • Your property administrator assignments are revoked
  • Audit trail references are retained with anonymized identifiers

Important: Account deletion is irreversible. If you need to raise a tenant management issue after deletion, please contact your estate’s body corporate directly.

7. Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encrypted passwords (bcrypt)
  • JWT-based session tokens stored securely on your device
  • Immutable audit logs that cannot be altered after creation
  • Role-based access controls enforcing least-privilege principles

8. Open Source

AnfieldVoice is built on open-source software. We believe in transparent, auditable technology. See our Open Source Notices for details.
